About Us

Based in Kakinada, the Sanjeev Group of Educational Institutions offer a comprehensive academic journey from school to postgraduate programs. For over a decade, we've focused on shaping future leaders with high-quality education, character building, and life skills. Our alumni's success reflect our commitment to excellence.

SAMVIDH is the highly anticipated Annual State-Level Degree College Event, organized by the Sanjeev Group of Colleges. It is a vibrant & robust platform that brings together approximately 900 (Nine hundred) young minds to showcase their talent, creativity, and innovation through a blend of technical and cultural events. SAMVIDH fosters a spirit of healthy competition and collaboration, providing a valuable opportunity for students to network, learn from their peers, and exhibit their skills to a wider audience. With an exciting mix of knowledge-driven contests, cultural showcases, and fun-filled activities, SAMVIDH-2025 promises to be an unforgettable celebration of youth and excellence. Sanjeev Group of Colleges is committed to making SAMVIDH a resounding success, contributing significantly to the holistic development of the participating students.
